Biography

My name is Tom Geheran. I am a senior mechanical engineering student at Tufts University
I grew up in New Hampshire with my mom, dad and sister. I had a fun childhood with lots of
lake swimming in the summer, sledding in the winter and sports during the school year.
In high school I started to snowboard which I fell in love with and I joined the Varsity
swimming and lacrosse teams. The end of senior year of high school I was named Prom King.
I graduated from high school in June of 2009 and went to Tufts that fall. Here I have met
friends I will have for the rest of my life and memories to match. I also learned a little
bit along the way as well.
